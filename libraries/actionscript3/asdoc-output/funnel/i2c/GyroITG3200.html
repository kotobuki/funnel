public function calibrate(totalSamples:uint):void

Routine to calibrate x, y, and z values to approximately zero. PLEASE NOTE: The calibration method may take several seconds to complete. Listen for GyroEvent.CALIBRATION_PROGRESS to get current progress, reading the progress property of the GyroEvent.

Parameters

totalSamples:uint (default = NaN) — total number of samples to use for calibration (default = 500)
